    Bachelor of Science in Office Administration



    Program Description
    The Bachelor of Science in Office Administration (BSOA) is a degree program designed to equip students with essential skills in office management, business communication, information technology, and human resource management. Its purpose is to develop students' competencies in administrative and clerical tasks, prepare them for careers as office managers, administrative assistants, and executive secretaries, and ensure proficiency in modern office technologies and software. By providing a strong foundation in business principles, BSOA graduates are well-prepared to contribute to the efficient operation of businesses and organizations, thereby supporting community development and economic growth.

    Bachelor of Science in Agri-Business


    Program Description
    The Bachelor of Science in Agribusiness (BS Agribusiness) is a degree program that integrates agricultural science with business management principles. It aims to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to manage agricultural enterprises effectively. The curriculum covers areas such as agricultural economics, farm management, marketing, finance, and rural development. The purpose of the program is to prepare students for careers in the agribusiness sector, including roles in farm management, agricultural marketing, and agribusiness entrepreneurship. By fostering expertise in both agriculture and business, BS Agribusiness graduates help improve agricultural productivity, promote sustainable farming practices, and contribute to the economic development of rural communities.

    Bachelor of Science in Public Administration


    Program Description
    The Bachelor of Public Administration (BPA) is a degree program focused on preparing students for careers in public service and administration. It encompasses the study of government policies, public sector management, organizational theory, and public finance. The purpose of the program is to develop skilled professionals who can effectively manage public resources, implement policies, and lead governmental and non-governmental organizations. BPA graduates play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of public services, promoting good governance, and contributing to community development and societal well-being.

    Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teacher Education


    Program Description
    The Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teacher Education (BTVTEd) is a degree program aimed at preparing students to become effective educators in the field of technical and vocational education and training (TVET). The program combines pedagogical training with technical skills development in areas such as automotive technology, electronics, computer technology, and other vocational disciplines. Its purpose is to equip future teachers with the knowledge and skills necessary to deliver high-quality technical education, develop curriculum, and implement instructional strategies tailored to vocational subjects. BTVTEd graduates play a vital role in enhancing the quality of vocational education, addressing skills gaps in the workforce, and contributing to the economic development of their communities by preparing students for various technical careers.

    Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship


    Program Description
    The Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship (BS Entrepreneurship) is a degree program designed to prepare students for launching and managing their own businesses. The curriculum includes courses in business planning, marketing, finance, innovation, and strategic management. The program's purpose is to foster entrepreneurial thinking and provide students with the practical skills needed to identify business opportunities, develop business plans, secure funding, and manage start-up ventures. BS Entrepreneurship graduates contribute to the community by driving economic growth, creating jobs, and introducing innovative products and services that meet the needs of society.

    Bachelor of Science in Industrial Security Management


    Program Description
    The Bachelor of Science in Industrial Security Management (BSISM) is a degree program designed to prepare students for careers in the field of security management within industrial and corporate settings. The curriculum covers topics such as risk management, physical security, cybersecurity, emergency planning, and loss prevention. The purpose of the program is to develop professionals who can effectively protect people, property, and information in various industrial environments. BSISM graduates help ensure the safety and security of industrial operations, contribute to the prevention of security breaches and incidents, and support overall organizational resilience and community safety.
